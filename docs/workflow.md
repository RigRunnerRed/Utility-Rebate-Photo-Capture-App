# User Workflow

## Office Workflow
1. Office staff creates a job entry prior to installation
2. Job includes address, project name, and PSE job ID
3. Job appears in the field crew mobile app
4. Office staff reviews submitted photos
5. Photos are used for PSE compliance submissions

## Field Crew Workflow
1. Crew member opens the mobile app
2. Crew selects the assigned job from a list
3. Crew takes required photos using the in-app camera
4. Crew submits photos
5. Confirmation is displayed and process is complete
